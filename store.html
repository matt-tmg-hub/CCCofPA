Questions, answered.

How does the deposit and final payment work?
Because pricing is based on actual packaged weight (which varies slightly from animal to animal), your reservation secures your share with a one-third deposit today. The remaining balance is based on the exact weight of your packaged beef and settled at pickup.
When will my beef be ready?
The 2026 harvest is expected to be ready in late October or the first week of November. We'll be in touch with exact timing and pickup coordination once the cattle go to the processor.
Do I have to deal with the butcher?
No. We process at a USDA-certified butcher in Lancaster County, handle pickup ourselves, and bring the finished beef back locally. You never need to coordinate anything with the processor.
What's the freezer situation for a whole share?
A whole share (~400 lbs packaged) needs real freezer space. That's why we include a chest freezer with every whole share purchase — plug it in and you're ready to go.
Can I customize the cuts?
Our cuts are predetermined to make sure each share is balanced and consistent across buyers. You'll get a well-rounded selection of steaks, roasts, ground beef, and everything in between — no guesswork required.
Where is pickup?
Pickup is coordinated locally in Pennsylvania after we bring the finished beef back from the Lancaster County processor. We'll confirm the exact pickup location and window with you directly.
