Our Story

A handful of cattle. A lot of care.

Cornerstone Cattle Company of PA is a small, family-owned operation in Bucks County, Pennsylvania, raising Black Angus beef the way it ought to be raised — slowly, on open pasture, with the whole family involved from fence line to freezer.

Our cattle spend their lives on grass, and are finished on grain at the end to develop the marbling, tenderness, and deep flavor you'd expect from premium beef. With a small Wagyu-cross influence in this year's herd, the eating quality is something we're genuinely proud of.

We process at a USDA-certified butcher in Lancaster County, handle pickup ourselves, and bring the finished beef back locally for you. No coordinating with the processor. No runaround. Just good beef, raised right, delivered by the people who raised it.
